How do I get CCleaner Support?

There are several ways to contact CCleaner’s support team depending on your issue and the version of CCleaner you’re using (Free or Professional). Here's a guide on how you can reach CCleaner customer support and what type of help you can expect:

1. Visit the Official Support Page

The fastest way to get help is by visiting the official support portal at Here, you’ll find FAQs, troubleshooting guides, software downloads, and contact options tailored to your query. It’s the go-to resource for basic to advanced help with CCleaner products.

2. Live Chat and Email Support

CCleaner also provides email and chat-based support for Pro and Business users. Through the website, users can access a live chat widget powered by Zendesk, which connects you with trained support agents for immediate assistance. Be sure to have your license key and registered email ready when contacting support.

3. Common Issues Solved by CCleaner Support

  • Installation or update problems
  • License activation errors
  • Product key recovery
  • Compatibility with antivirus or Windows updates
  • Crash or performance issues

4. How to Retrieve Your License Information

If you’ve lost your license or have trouble activating your software, CCleaner allows users to retrieve their product key using the email address registered at the time of purchase. Visit the "Lost License" section on their support page and follow the prompts.

5. Is There a CCleaner Customer Support Phone Number?

As of now, CCleaner does not offer direct phone support for most regions. All support is handled via email, live chat, or through their web-based help center. This ensures all communications are logged and managed securely through the Zendesk platform.

6. Help for Free Version Users

Users of the free version of CCleaner can access the community forum and knowledge base. While live support isn’t available for free users, most common questions are covered through detailed articles and user discussions. The support portal is updated regularly to address issues related to the free version.

7. Tips Before Contacting Support

Before reaching out, make sure to:

  • Check the FAQs and knowledge base for quick answers
  • Have your version number, OS version, and license key ready
  • Reboot your PC after updates or installations
  • Temporarily disable antivirus to check for software conflicts
